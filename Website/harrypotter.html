Short Description of Harry Potter Series

Harry Potter is a series of seven fantasy novels written by British author J.K. Rowling. the novels Chronicle the lives of a young wizard, Harry Potter, and his friends Hermione Granger and Ron Weasly, all of whom are students at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. The main story are concerns Harry's struggle against Lord Voldermort, a dark wizard who intends to become immortal, overthrow the wizard governing body known as the Ministry of Magic and subjugate all wizards and Muggles(non-magical people.)

Order of Books

  2. The Chamber of Secrets(1998)
  3. The Prisoner of Azkaban(1999)
  4. The Goblet of friends(2000)
  5. The Order of the Phoenix(2003)
  6. The Half-Blood Prince(2005)
  7. The Deathly Hallows(2007)
